## Design Context

### Users
Israelis moving to a new apartment — anxious, time-pressured, often dealing with unfamiliar bureaucracy.
Primary use: desktop/laptop during work hours, filling out forms and uploading documents.
Secondary use: mobile phone while on the go, checking task status.

### Brand Personality
Three words: **reliable · clear · official-but-better**
Emotional goal: give the user confidence and a sense of control over a stressful process.
Tone: calm authority — like a knowledgeable friend who has been through this before.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme:** Light mode — user is stressed, needs clarity and calm (not drama)
- **Primary color:** Deep navy oklch(25% 0.08 245) — trust, authority, official
- **Accent:** Muted teal oklch(52% 0.12 200) — action, progress
- **Surfaces:** Navy-tinted whites — cohesive, not clinical
- **Typography:** Frank Ruhl Libre headings + Heebo body (full Hebrew support)
- **Anti-references:** NOT a startup landing page, NOT playful/childish, NOT generic SaaS dashboard

### Design Principles
1. One primary action per screen
2. Status always visible
3. Official but warmer
4. RTL-first
5. Empty states teach
